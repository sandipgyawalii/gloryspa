Swedish Massage

Rs. 85 - Rs. 120

A gentle, relaxing massage that uses long strokes, kneading, and circular movements to ease tension and promote relaxation.

Benefits
  • Reduces stress and anxiety
  • Improves circulation
  • Relieves muscle tension and pain
  • Promotes better sleep
  • Enhances overall well-being
What to Expect

During your Swedish massage, our skilled therapist will use a combination of techniques including long, flowing strokes, kneading, and gentle circular movements. The pressure can be adjusted to your preference. The session begins with a brief consultation to address any concerns or specific areas that need attention.

Duration

60/90 minutes

Recommended For

All clients, especially first-time spa-goers
