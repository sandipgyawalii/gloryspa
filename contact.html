Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

You can book an appointment in several ways: online through our website, by calling our booking line at (555) 123-4568, or by visiting our spa in person. We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance to ensure availability, especially for weekend appointments.

We require 24 hours' notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service price. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.

Yes, we offer gift certificates for all our services and packages. Gift certificates can be purchased online, over the phone, or in person at our spa. They are valid for one year from the date of purchase and can be personalized with a special message.

We recommend arriving 15-20 minutes before your scheduled appointment to complete any necessary paperwork and begin your relaxation journey. Please avoid heavy meals and alcohol consumption before your treatment. For massage services, it's best to avoid shaving on the day of your appointment.

Yes, we offer a membership program that provides discounted rates on services, priority booking, and exclusive member-only promotions. We also have a loyalty program where you earn points for every dollar spent, which can be redeemed for services or products. Ask our front desk staff for more details on these programs.
